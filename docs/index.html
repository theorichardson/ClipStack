ClipStack

A quiet little menu-bar utility for your Mac.

Download for macOS

Clipboard history

Open with ⌘⇧X. Filter by app, search, and paste from your Mac or iPhone.

Region & window capture

Screenshot or record any region or window from the menu bar. Save presets for repeat grabs.

Framed exports

Frame captures on macOS wallpapers before copying or dragging them out.

Width presets

Resize the frontmost window to a saved width from the menu bar or ⌘⇧1–9.
